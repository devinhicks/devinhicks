Tiny Planet Apothecary

Let your potions propel you!

Tiny Planet Apothecary is a task-driven potion making and exploration game set on a very small planet in a star system full of magic. The player controls a human character of their own design, and explores the different areas of their little home planet, collecting different ingredients to farm and craft potions with.

For my senior capstone project (conducted over the course of two semesters), my two teammates and I created Tiny Planet Apothecary in Unity/C#. I was responsible primarily for UI design and implementation, character rigging, and environment asset development, and worked with my team on the overall game design process. My team and I worked through the challenges of developing an ambitious game in a very tight timeframe, all while each individually taking on roles and responsibilities that were entirely new to us. You can read more about our full development processhere, or read on to learn about my contributions to the project.

UI Design & Implementation

  • Gathered references for UI designs from games like Breath of the Wild, Stardew Valley, and Genshin Impact
  • Created wireframes and concepts for menus and HUD
  • Iterated over layouts and designs on paper and in Photoshop
  • Worked closely with programmer to ensure functionality of the UI within existing code
  • Implemented the final designs in a collaborative Unity project
  • Created UI assets in Photoshop from my own designs, as well as icons based on concepts by the lead artist

3D Asset Creation & Character Rigging

  • Gathered references for vision boards to inspire mood and style of the game
  • Created and itereated over concept art for the player's cottage, then modeled and textured the exterior and interior spaces
  • Modeled and textured farming ingredients and character customizations based on concepts created by the lead artist
  • Retopologized, rigged, and created controls for both the human player character and the player companion character, an octopus named Tako

Game Design

  • In collaboartion with team, iterated over gameplay mechanics and features
  • Playtested mechanics using paper prototypes and refined until better balanced
  • Collaborated on narrative and character design with lead artist
  • Scripted some simple gameplay element and companion character customization system
  • Collaboraatively documented progress during production using Google Docs and summarized into game design document
